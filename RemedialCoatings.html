Remedial Coatings LLC in Colorado Springs, Colorado, stands at the forefront of decorative concrete and custom epoxy coatings. Founded By Michael Thomas Horner in 2020, Remedial Coatings evolved from its origins as Springs Clean & Seal, Springs Crete & Seal, and later back to, Springs Clean & Seal, established in 2011 founded by Michael Horner. Later in December of 2022, Michael would go on to add his brother Jay Morlock as a senior executive for Remedial Coatings, adding his strength, administration, experience, knowledge, expertise and love to the organization.

Through out the years the team has earned a reputation for exceptional craftsmanship and an unwavering commitment to excellence. While we are not accredited with the Better Business Bureau (BBB) at this moment, we have maintained BBB accreditation through various periods over the years.
